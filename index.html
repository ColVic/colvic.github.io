What we are

Main activity segments

English Taught Honour Programme (FAF) is widely popular for its involvment in activities for students from Techincal University of Moldova as well as the rest of local IT community.

IT Department

Being the unique English Taught group of the Computer Science Department, FAF has an acknowledged reputation of high-skilled engineers on national and international IT market.

Community

Along years full of traditional events, teambuildings and public achievements, FAFers from all generations got to know each other from personal and professional points of view.

NGO

Having reached the peak of gratitude from University's administration, FAF decided to officialize its activity by registering as a Non-Guvernamental Organization.
